About Company

Savaj Industries is a leading metal casting company specializing in high quality mould casting solutions for a diverse range of industries.

The company excels in creating precision-engineered castings using advanced equipment like the Sand Plant ARPA-450 and Electrotherm induction furnaces. Backed by a CNC machine workshop and sophisticated laboratory testing tools from Versatile Equipment LTD, the company ensures unparalleled accuracy and quality in its castings.

Savaj Industries serves industries with customized casting solutions, meeting the highest standards of durability and performance.

At Savaj Industries,we strive for excellence and thrive in achieving it.

The Synergy Of Leadership

Team Savaj brings together a wealth of techno-commercial expertise, supported by a dedicated team of engineers specializing in development and quality assurance.

The Machine Capabilities

At Savaj, we prioritize continuous capital investment in the latest machinery to serve our esteemed clients without compromising on quality. We are committed to developing in-house solutions for value-added processes such as machining and radiography (NDT), ensuring the timely delivery of our machined parts.

OUR FACILITIES

The facilities are equipped with state-of-the-art equipment, including the Sand Plant ARPA-450 and our custom mold handling system. We also have an induction furnace manufacturing all cast iron products. In the same building, a CNC machine workshop is used for both threading and machining processes. Additionally, our laboratory is outtted with advanced sand-testing equipment from Versatile Equipment LTD, along with a hardness tester, spectrometer, and microscope for thorough product testing before shipment.
